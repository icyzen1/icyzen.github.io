About Us

Our complex is more than just a rental property. We have created a space for those who appreciate silence, crystal clear mountain air and home comfort even far from the city. Our wooden cottages are designed so that you can enjoy panoramic mountain views right from your living room window.

Each house is equipped with everything you need for an unforgettable vacation: from cozy fireplaces that warm you on winter evenings, to modern kitchens and comfortable bedrooms. We make sure that your stay in the mountains is filled with only peace and positive emotions. Come for inspiration and the rest you deserve.
